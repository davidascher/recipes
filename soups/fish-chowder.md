# Fish Chowder

## Ingredients

* 2 slices bacon, chopped
* 1 medium onion, chopped \(1/2 c\)
* 1 c sliced celery
* 2 large tomatoes, peeled, diced \(1c\)
* 1 large potato, diced \(1c\)
* 1 lb fish
* 2 1/2c water
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp pepper
* 3 Tbsp butter
* 1/4 cup flour
* 2 cup milk

## Steps

* Cook bacon, then onion.
* Add water, veggies
* Cook potato
* Add fish
* Make white sauce separately, then add.
